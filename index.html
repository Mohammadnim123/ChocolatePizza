Forthe fig-swirl: Melt butter over medium heat in a saucepan. Add brown sugar and stir to dissolve. Halve all of the figs and toss in the saucepan with water and lemon juice. Cookover medium heat stirring frequently, until you have a chunky-jammymixture. Add salt with one or two stirs, set aside and let cool completely.
